Reading

  • Moral Tribes Joshua Greene

  • The Righteous Mind & The Happiness Hypothesis Jonathon Haidt

  • Just Babies & Against Empathy Paul Bloom

  • Justice: What’s the right thing to do? Michael Sandel

  • What It Means to Be Moral Phil Zuckerman
